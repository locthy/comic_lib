A) Comic downloader:
    - Get comic list by searching names in other comic web.
    - After selecting comic, choose the start chapter and end chapter for downloading.
    - Download the comic data: description, chapter time download, author, ...
    - Ensure the download comic pages by two sum check.

B) Server comic:
    - A homepage contains comics thumbnails and a search bar, sort bar (by genre, comic chaps, latest update) ascending, descending.
    - Having the authentication and authorization for comments and rating.	
    - In chapter list, there is a description, tags, state, rating, choosing chapter.
    - In chapter, the page contains pages comic and in the end is the comment section. 

C) Database:
    - Store comics data.

Non-functional requirement:
    - The server must response under 1 second.
